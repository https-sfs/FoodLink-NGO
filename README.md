**FoodLink**

FoodLink is a community-driven platform designed to reduce food waste and improve food accessibility by connecting **food donors, NGOs, and communities** through technology.

Built with a strong focus on **social impact**, FoodLink enables collaboration between individuals and organizations to ensure surplus food reaches those who need it the most.

---

**Problem Statement:**
Large amounts of food are wasted every day, while many communities and individuals still struggle with food insecurity.  
Existing solutions often lack seamless coordination between donors, NGOs, and volunteers.

---

**Our Solution**
FoodLink acts as a **digital bridge** between:
- Food donors
- NGOs
- Community volunteers

By centralizing food sharing and NGO partnerships in one platform, FoodLink makes food redistribution **efficient, transparent, and scalable**.

---

**Key Features**
- **Food Sharing Platform** – Enables listing and discovery of available food resources  
- **Partner with Us as an NGO** – NGOs can register and collaborate for food distribution  
- **Community Impact Focused Design** – Built to support social good initiatives  
- **Modern UI** – Clean, responsive interface for smooth user experience  

---

**NGO Partnership Feature**
The **“Partner with Us as an NGO”** feature allows non-profit organizations to:
- Register on the platform
- Collaborate with donors and volunteers
- Coordinate food distribution efforts
- Expand their reach using technology

This feature strengthens FoodLink’s mission by directly involving NGOs in the food redistribution ecosystem.
